Cooking Instructions
  1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Pour sauce at the base of the pan
  3. Layer the pasta noodles with sauce inbetween each layer
  4. Apply the ground beef after defrosting
  5. Cook for approx. 3 hours
